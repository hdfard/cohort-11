Why study with us?

Danger!
Yea, you could go to a normal school and feel all safe.. Or you could come here where we will actively throw you in to life threatening situations!
We will ease you in gently, where you will enjoy the delights of backfiring spells exploding in your face. From there we will progress you to fighting an assortment of dangerous creatures. You may even get the opportunity to risk your life against

Nature! Do you like nature? Great! As you walk around our grounds you will quickly learn that nature here doesn't like you!
Get up close and personal with our willow tree and discover that you never truly get a day off from threatening situations around here.

The Chosen One Package
If you need more, don't worry - we got you!
Upgrade to our chosen one package and we will provide you with your very own arch enemy who will stop at nothing to eviscerate you!
You'll also have an amazing time learning politics as your enemy takes control of the government and turns the whole world against you!
Still not enough?! Well, if you hang on in there you will finally get a boss battle where the stakes are legitimately high. How cool is that?!
